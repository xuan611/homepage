Short Bio

I am an MSc student at the Nanyang Technological University advised by Prof. Bihan Wen (2021-now). Previously, I completed my Batchelor's degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University advised by Prof. Xiaojun Yu (2017-2021).

I am broadly interested in image processing, image analysis, computer vision and related problems in machine learning. My previous research focuses on image processing and analysis for the medical images. Currently, I'm particularly interested in inverse problems of image processing, especially the unsupervised learning for image restoration and real-world noise modeling.
